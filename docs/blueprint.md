# **App Name**: LTDK Alumni Hub

## Core Features:

- Informational Pages: Display key information about the Lyc√©e Technique Dan Kassawa, including its history, mission, values, and infrastructure.
- Course Catalog: Provide a comprehensive listing of available programs, complete with objectives, career paths, and testimonials.
- Alumni Network: Facilitate networking among former students through profiles, directories, and a members-only area. Features include alumni spotlights and success stories.
- Digital Library: Host past technical baccalaureate exam papers for download and study, enhancing access to educational resources. Also offers means for alumni and instructors to upload resources to this tool.
- Student Zone: Offer tips for academic and professional success, a 'Student of the Month' feature, and multimedia showcasing club activities to motivate current students.
- Contact and Location: Present detailed contact information, including addresses, phone numbers, and a Google Maps integration, ensuring accessibility for all users.
- Alumni Relations Chatbot: Use an AI chatbot that uses information provided in alumni profiles and school historical data, to respond to simple user questions, give history about alumni, or the school in general

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Navy blue (#1E3A8A), reflecting the technical nature of the institution, and reliability. It provides a strong, trustworthy base for the design.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F9FF), almost white, but adds a hint of color interest without distracting from the content.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#D4AC0D), drawn from the Niger flag and symbolizing the value and quality of education offered.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', sans-serif, offering a neutral and modern feel.
- Use clean, modern icons related to education, technology, and community, in the golden yellow to ensure they are noticed. Use icons sparingly.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout to ensure information is easily accessible and digestible. The design should focus on usability and reflect the institution's professionalism.
- Use subtle, non-intrusive animations for transitions and interactions to enhance user experience. Animations should be used to guide users, not distract them.
